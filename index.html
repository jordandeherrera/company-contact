Opt-In Requirements

To ensure compliance with Twilio's policies, the following opt-in requirements must be met:

  • Consent: Users must provide explicit consent to receive messages. This can be done via e-mail to the e-mail address listed above this text.
  • Clear Information: Users must be informed about the nature of the messages they will receive, including message frequency, costs, and how to opt-out. Message frequency is typically one to three times per week and carrier data rates may apply to messages. Opt-out can be achieved through either e-mail or by replying STOP.
  • Opt-Out Mechanism: Users must have a clear and easy way to opt-out of receiving messages. This can be done by replying with "STOP" or using another simple method.
  • Documentation: Euclid Bing maintains records of user consent and provides them to Twilio if requested.
