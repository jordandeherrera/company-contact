About Euclid Bing

Euclid Bing is an SMS messaging service that delivers real-time notifications on technical indicators for selected US equities, ensuring traders receive crucial updates instantly. Text delivery is necessary for its immediacy and reliability, allowing users to act quickly on market changes, regardless of their location or internet connectivity. This service provides a seamless way to stay informed and make timely trading decisions, enhancing overall trading efficiency and responsiveness.

Opt-In Disclosures

Euclid Bing uses Twilio for its SMS message delivery. To ensure compliance with Twilio's policies, the following opt-in policies are followed by Euclid Bing:

  • Consent: Users must provide explicit consent to receive messages (this can only be done by e-mail at this time).
  • Clear Information: Users are informed about the nature of the messages they will receive, including message frequency, costs, and how to opt-out. Message frequency is typically one to three times per week and carrier data rates may apply to messages. Opt-out can be achieved through either e-mail or by replying STOP.
  • Opt-Out Mechanism: Users must have a clear and easy way to opt-out of receiving messages. This can be done by replying with "STOP" or sending an opt-out request via e-mail.
  • Documentation: Euclid Bing maintains records of user consent (via e-mail) and will provide them to Twilio if requested.

Opt-In Acceptance

By providing your contact information via e-mail, you agree to receive event-based messages from Euclid Bing. Message frequency may vary. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to unsubscribe.
